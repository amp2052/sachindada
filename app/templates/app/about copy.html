Political Journey & Achievements

Sanjay Rathod is a prominent Shiv Sena politician from Maharashtra, representing the Digras Assembly Constituency in the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. He began his political journey at the age of 21 with the Shiv Shakti Organisation and later became active in Shiv Sena in the mid-1990s. In 1998–1999, he was appointed as the Yavatmal district chief of Shiv Sena by Balasaheb Thackeray. He was first elected as an MLA in 2004 from the Darwha constituency, which ceased to exist from 2008. Subsequently, he was elected from the Digras constituency in 2009, 2014, 2019, and 2024 Wikipedia.

In December 2014, Rathod was appointed as a state minister in the Maharashtra government, handling the revenue portfolio and serving as the guardian minister of Yavatmal district Bharatpedia. He was later made a cabinet minister on 30 December 2019.

Rathod belongs to the Banjara community, a significant vote bank in Maharashtra, particularly in the Vidarbha region. He is known for his aggressive political style and has a large following in the Banjara community.
